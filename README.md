The "Daasoham" channel on YouTube is a popular digital platform dedicated to Indian spiritual music, specifically Carnatic devotional songs, shlokas, and traditional recitals tied to the Madhva/Dvaita traditions.
The term "Daasoham" loosely translates to "I am a servant [of the Lord]", referencing Hanuman's iconic introduction to Sri Rama (Daasoham Kosalendrasya).
